Who We Are

Redefining the Design-Build Landscape in Saudi Arabia

At FurnishIQ, we believe a space is more than a physical structure. It is a thoughtful, livable environment — one that should be as comfortable as it is inspiring. We move beyond conventional design limitations to deliver personalised solutions for homeowners, businesses, and organisations across the Kingdom, fusing creativity with operational excellence.

FurnishIQ is a Saudi-based design-build company specialising in interior design, turnkey fit-out, furniture solutions, and project management. From concept development and engineering to procurement, execution, and handover, our multidisciplinary team delivers seamless project experiences through innovation, quality craftsmanship, and disciplined project management.

Our Commitments

The FurnishIQ Edge

01

Visual Certainty Through Photorealism

We eliminate the guesswork of construction. By providing full photoreal 3D renders of every floor and landscape before a single brick is laid, we empower clients to decide with absolute confidence — approving textures, lighting, and layouts in a virtual environment.

02

Strategic DNA & Brand-Led Planning

Design should serve a purpose. Our approach incorporates brand-led planning, where every layout and traffic flow is strategically engineered to reflect your unique identity and optimise daily operations — balancing high-end aesthetics with practical, high-performance functionality.

03

The Zero-Snag Promise

Excellence in execution is our hallmark. Through rigorous Quality Assurance (QA/QC) and continuous Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) monitoring, we guarantee a clean build — ensuring your space is ready for immediate use without the stress of lingering technical issues.
