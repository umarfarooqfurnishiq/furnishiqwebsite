Jeddah Boutique Hotel

Redefining commercial hospitality through a full Design–Build partnership, from concept to guest-ready handover.

The Brief

A Boutique Identity for a Commercial Address

The client sought to convert a raw commercial shell in central Jeddah into an intimate, design-forward boutique hotel — one that would compete with international hospitality brands while reflecting a distinctly Saudi sense of place.

FurnishIQ delivered the project end-to-end: spatial planning and interior design, full MEP engineering, structural fit-out, and bespoke furniture procurement — all under a single point of accountability, with photoreal 3D visuals approved before construction began.
